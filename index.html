What is a meme token?

Memecoins are a type of cryptocurrency that are often inspired by internet memes, characters, or trends. They are typically supported by enthusiastic online communities and are generally intended to be light-hearted and fun.

Examples of memecoins include Dogecoin, Shiba Inu, and others that are often associated with entertainment rather than usability. Despite their playful nature, it's important to understand that memecoins are highly speculative and may hold little or no intrinsic value.
